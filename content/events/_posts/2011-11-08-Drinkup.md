The next Ruby Drink-up of Sophia is to take place on
__Tuesday__, the __8th of November__ at __6:30pm__ at the __Green King__ pub, 
[espace Saint-Philippe](http://www.openstreetmap.org/?lat=43.618463&lon=7.0753385&zoom=18&layers=M&mlat=43.61858&mlon=7.07508),
Sophia Antipolis.

We are pleased to announce two mini-talks (15 minutes each) for this edition:

* *Simple web-scraping with [Mechanize](http://mechanize.rubyforge.org/) and
  [Nokogiri](http://nokogiri.org/)* by [Muriel Salvan](http://twitter.com/MurielSalvan);
* *Universal networking in Ruby with [ZeroMQ](http://www.zeromq.org/)* by [Emmanuel Bastien](http://twitter.com/ebastien).

Join us to chat over a drink and meet with the Ruby community of Sophia.
