The November edition of the Ruby Drink-up of Sophia took place on
the 8th of November at the __Green King__ pub, 
[espace Saint-Philippe](http://www.openstreetmap.org/?lat=43.618463&lon=7.0753385&zoom=18&layers=M&mlat=43.61858&mlon=7.07508),
Sophia Antipolis.

We got two mini-talks:

* *[Simple web-scraping with Mechanize and Nokogiri](http://www.slideshare.net/rivierarb/mechanize-at-the-ruby-drinkup-of-sophia-november-2011)* by [Muriel Salvan](http://twitter.com/MurielSalvan);
* *[Universal networking in Ruby with ZeroMQ](http://ebastien.github.com/rds-talk-zeromq)* by [Emmanuel Bastien](http://twitter.com/ebastien).

Feel free to share the presentations and contact the speakers on Twitter for more information.
