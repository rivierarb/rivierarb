The next Ruby Drink-up of Sophia is to take place on
__Tuesday__, the __24th of September__ at __6:30pm__ at the __Green King__ pub,
[espace Saint-Philippe](http://www.openstreetmap.org/?lat=43.618463&lon=7.0753385&zoom=18&layers=M&mlat=43.61858&mlon=7.07508),
Sophia Antipolis.

We have one presentation on the agenda:

* *Introduction to [Elixir](http://elixir-lang.org/)* by [Nicolas Bousquet](http://twitter.com/bousquetn).

  Nicolas will talk about [Elixir](http://elixir-lang.org/), a modern and functional programming language running on top of the [Erlang](http://www.erlang.org/) virtual machine.

Join us to chat over a drink and meet with the Ruby community of Sophia!
