On __Friday__, the __13th of April__ at __6:30pm__ in Nice, [12 Boulevard de l'Armée des Alpes](http://www.openstreetmap.org/?lat=43.707382&lon=7.292639&zoom=18&layers=M),
__[Les Satellites](http://www.satellites-teletravail.com/)__ and __[Riviera.rb](http://rivierarb.fr/)__ were proud to
host an amazing talk from [Shannon -jj Behrens](http://jjinux.blogspot.com/), developer advocate at Google and specialized in YouTube APIs.

<p><a href="http://www.satellites-teletravail.com/" title="Les Satellites - Télétravail et Coworking, Nice, Sophia, Monaco, Cannes"><img alt="Les Satellites" src="http://www.satellites-teletravail.com/assets/pict/logo.png"/></a></p>

Taking into account the mixed audience of Ruby developers and social media experts, [Shannon -jj Behrens](http://jjinux.blogspot.com/), gave a top-to-bottom talk starting from explaining why YouTube is a great tool to grow a business and ending with a more technical speech on the integration of YouTube APIs in modern web frameworks.

Greetings to __[Les Satellites](http://www.satellites-teletravail.com/)__ for organizing such a friendly event,
and to __JJ__ for taking the time in his tight schedule to stop by Nice to give a very interesting talk.

* __About JJ__

JJ is a developer advocate for YouTube APIs. His goal is to foster a rich set of third-party applications built on YouTube APIs. He's a well-known member of the Python community. He blogs at [http://jjinux.blogspot.com](http://jjinux.blogspot.com) on topics such as Python, Ruby, Linux, open source software, the Web, and lesser-known programming languages.
