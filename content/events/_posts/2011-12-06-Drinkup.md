The last Ruby drink-up of year 2011 took place on
the 6th of December at the __Green King__ pub, 
[espace Saint-Philippe](http://www.openstreetmap.org/?lat=43.618463&lon=7.0753385&zoom=18&layers=M&mlat=43.61858&mlon=7.07508),
Sophia Antipolis.

We got two mini-talks:

* *[The Dark Side of Programming Languages](http://www.slideshare.net/jbmazon/the-dark-side-of-programming-languages)* by [Jean-Baptiste Mazon](http://twitter.com/jbmazon);
* *[DRb: an easy distributed objects framework for Ruby](http://www.slideshare.net/rivierarb/drb-at-the-ruby-drinkup-of-sophia-december-2011)* by [Nicolas Bondoux](http://twitter.com/nicolas_bondoux).

Feel free to share the presentations and contact the speakers on Twitter for more information.
