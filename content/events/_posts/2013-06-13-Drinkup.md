The next Ruby Drink-up of Sophia is to take place on
__Thursday__, the __13th of June__ at __6:30pm__ at the __Green King__ pub,
[espace Saint-Philippe](http://www.openstreetmap.org/?lat=43.618463&lon=7.0753385&zoom=18&layers=M&mlat=43.61858&mlon=7.07508),
Sophia Antipolis.

We have one presentation on the agenda:

* *[A JSON API to bind them all](http://ebastien.github.io/rds-talk-jsonapi/)* by [Emmanuel Bastien](http://twitter.com/ebastien).

  Emmanuel will talk about the new [JSON API](http://jsonapi.org/) specification that aims to standardize JSON-based protocols between your favorite web frameworks.

Join us to chat over a drink and meet with the Ruby community of Sophia!
