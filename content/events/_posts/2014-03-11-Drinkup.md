Our next Ruby Drink-up in Sophia-Antipolis is to take place on
__Tuesday__, the __11th of March__ at __6:30pm__ at the __Green King__ pub,
[espace Saint-Philippe](http://www.openstreetmap.org/?lat=43.618463&lon=7.0753385&zoom=18&layers=M&mlat=43.61858&mlon=7.07508),

We have a great presentation on the agenda:

* *Building a FirefoxOS mobile application using EmberJS*, presented by Tobias Rothe.

  Tobias will make a short presentation on [FirefoxOS](http://www.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/os/), then show us how to make a mobile app in HTML5, illustrated with an example written using [EmberJS](http://emberjs.com/). Don't miss that!

Join us to chat over a drink and meet with the Ruby community of Sophia-Antipolis!
