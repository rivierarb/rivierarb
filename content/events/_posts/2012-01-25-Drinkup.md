The first Ruby Drink-up of 2012 took place on
the 25th of January at the __Green King__ pub, 
[espace Saint-Philippe](http://www.openstreetmap.org/?lat=43.618463&lon=7.0753385&zoom=18&layers=M&mlat=43.61858&mlon=7.07508),
Sophia Antipolis.

We got two mini-talks:

* *[Goliath, a tiny GIANT](http://sleeper.github.com/goliath-presentation/goliath.html)* by [Frédérick Ros](http://twitter.com/sl33p3r);
* *[Piloting processes in real time through standard I/O using Ruby](http://www.slideshare.net/rivierarb/piloting-processes-through-std-io-at-the-ruby-drinkup-of-sophia-january-2012)* by [Muriel Salvan](http://twitter.com/MurielSalvan).

Feel free to share the presentations and contact the speakers on Twitter for more information.
