The October edition of the Ruby Drink-up of Sophia took place on
Tuesday the 4th, at the __Green King__ pub, 
[espace Saint-Philippe](http://www.openstreetmap.org/?lat=43.618463&lon=7.0753385&zoom=18&layers=M&mlat=43.61858&mlon=7.07508),
Sophia Antipolis.

We got two enlightening mini-talks:

* *[MetaWhere, stéroïdes pour ActiveRecord](http://www.slideshare.net/rivierarb/metawhere-at-the-ruby-drinkup-of-sophia-october-2011)*,
  by [Pierre Schambacher](http://twitter.com/PierreSchambac);
* *[Amber](http://amber-lang.net): there's an IDE in my browser*,
  by [Geoffroy Couprie](http://twitter.com/gcouprie).

[Nicolas Dupont](http://twitter.com/lowx512) and [Nima Izadi ](http://twitter.com/Nima_Izadi)
improvised a presentation of two of their projects:

* [CoffeeTouch](https://github.com/Crozis/CoffeeTouch), an open-source Multi-touch JavaScript Library;
* [On The Rails Again](http://ontherailsagain.com/), a new blog targeting Ruby on Rails beginners.

Feel free to share the presentations and contact the speakers on Twitter for more information.
