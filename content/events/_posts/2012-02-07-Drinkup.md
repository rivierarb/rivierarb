The Ruby Drink-up of February 2012 took place on
the 7th at the __Green King__ pub, 
[espace Saint-Philippe](http://www.openstreetmap.org/?lat=43.618463&lon=7.0753385&zoom=18&layers=M&mlat=43.61858&mlon=7.07508),
Sophia Antipolis.

We got two mini-talks:

* *[CoffeeScript](http://coffeescript.org/)* by [Evan Genieur](http://twitter.com/evangenieur);
* *[Pry, a quick overview](http://www.slideshare.net/rivierarb/pry-at-the-ruby-drinkup-of-sophia-february-2012)* by [Philippe Antras](http://twitter.com/_philant_).

Feel free to share the presentations and contact the speakers on Twitter for more information.
