The back-to-school edition of the Ruby Drink-up of Sophia took place on
__Tuesday__, the __6th of September__ at __6:30pm__ at the __Green King__ pub, 
[espace Saint-Philippe](http://www.openstreetmap.org/?lat=43.618463&lon=7.0753385&zoom=18&layers=M&mlat=43.61858&mlon=7.07508),
Sophia Antipolis.

We were fifteen to attend this edition and we got two mini-talks:

* [FPM: the Swiss Army knife of the casual packager](http://ebastien.github.com/rds-talk-fpm)
  by Emmanuel Bastien ([@ebastien](http://twitter.com/ebastien))
* [Ruby in the context of a music production toolchain](http://www.slideshare.net/rivierarb/music-production-tools-at-the-ruby-drinkup-of-sophia-september-2011)
  by Muriel Salvan ([@MurielSalvan](http://twitter.com/MurielSalvan))

Feel free to share the presentations and to contact the speakers on Twitter for more information.
