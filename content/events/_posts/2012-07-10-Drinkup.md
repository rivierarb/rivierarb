The next Ruby Drink-up of Sophia is to take place on
__Tuesday__, the __10th of July__ at __6:30pm__ at the __Green King__ pub, 
[espace Saint-Philippe](http://www.openstreetmap.org/?lat=43.618463&lon=7.0753385&zoom=18&layers=M&mlat=43.61858&mlon=7.07508),
Sophia Antipolis.

The whole session will be dedicated to two subjects:

* Typesafe web programming with [Haskell](http://haskell.org/) and [Yesod](http://www.yesodweb.com/), by [Jean-Baptiste Mazon](http://twitter.com/jbmazon)
* Advanced [Raspberry Pi](http://raspberrypi.org/) and [Arduino](http://www.arduino.cc/) hacking, by [Nono](http://twitter.com/NonoPobot) from [PoBot](http://pobot.org/), the Sophia Antipolis robotics club

Join us to chat over a drink and meet with the Ruby community of Sophia.
