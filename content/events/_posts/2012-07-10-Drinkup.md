The Ruby Drink-up of July 2012 took place on Tuesday the 10th of July
at the __Green King__ pub, [espace Saint-Philippe](http://www.openstreetmap.org/?lat=43.618463&lon=7.0753385&zoom=18&layers=M&mlat=43.61858&mlon=7.07508),
Sophia Antipolis.

The following two subjects were tackled:

* Typesafe web programming with [Haskell](http://haskell.org/) and [Yesod](http://www.yesodweb.com/), by [Jean-Baptiste Mazon](http://twitter.com/jbmazon)

* [Advanced Raspberry Pi and Arduino
  hacking](http://www.slideshare.net/rivierarb/presentation-ruby-nono), by
  [Nono](http://twitter.com/NonoPobot) and
  [Julien Holtzer](http://twitter.com/JulienPobot) from
  [PoBot](http://pobot.org/), the Sophia Antipolis robotics club.
  [further information about [Raspberry Pi](http://raspberrypi.org/)
  and [Arduino](http://www.arduino.cc/)]

Feel free to share the presentations and contact the speakers on
Twitter for more information.
